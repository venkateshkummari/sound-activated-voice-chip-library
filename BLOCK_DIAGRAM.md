
# Block Diagram

The system consists of the following blocks:

1. Condenser Microphone
2. Microphone Pre-Amplifier
3. Audio Amplifier
4. Op-Amp Voltage Comparator
5. Microcontroller (89C2051)
6. Voice Record and Playback Chip
7. Speaker
8. Power Supply (+5V)

**Flow**:

Sound → Microphone → Pre-Amplifier → Audio Amplifier → 
Comparator → Microcontroller → Voice Chip → Speaker
