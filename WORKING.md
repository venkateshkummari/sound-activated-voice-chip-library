
# Working Principle

1. The condenser microphone detects sound signals from the environment.
2. The microphone output signal is very small, so it is amplified using a pre-amplifier.
3. The amplified signal is further boosted using an audio amplifier.
4. The signal is converted from analog to digital using an op-amp configured as a voltage comparator.
5. When the sound exceeds a predefined threshold level, the comparator outputs a HIGH signal.
6. The microcontroller detects this HIGH signal.
7. The microcontroller activates the voice playback chip.
8. The speaker plays a recorded message requesting people to maintain silence.
